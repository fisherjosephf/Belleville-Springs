Design and Analyze Disc Springs for Pressure Regulators

About Belleville Springs in Pressure Regulators

Belleville springs (disc springs) are conical washers widely used in pressure regulators to provide precise load characteristics. They offer high load capacity in a small space and can be stacked in series or parallel to achieve desired load-deflection characteristics. This calculator helps you design the optimal spring configuration for your pressure regulator application.
